Lasagna Recipes

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is a crowd-pleaser, featuring layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and gooey cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Egg
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add marinara sauce to the beef and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  6. Top with remaining mozzarella cheese.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes.
  8. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
