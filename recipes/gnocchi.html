Gnocchi Recipe

Description

This gnocchi recipe is a comforting dish featuring tender potato gnocchi tossed in a buttery garlic sauce and finished with fresh sage.

Ingredients

  • Potato gnocchi
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh sage
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook gnocchi according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add cooked gnocchi and toss to coat.
  4. Stir in Parmesan cheese, fresh sage, salt, and pepper.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with extra cheese and sage.
