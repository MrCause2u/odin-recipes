Spaghetti Recipe

Description

This spaghetti recipe features a rich and savory meat sauce served over al dente noodles, topped with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add canned tomatoes and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in fresh basil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Combine sauce with drained spaghetti and serve.
